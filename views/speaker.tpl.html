Felix Wahner

Felix is CTO at the Hannover / Germany based communication agency neuwaerts

Felix started falling in love with the web back in the days before the first great browser war between Netscape and Microsoft was about to rise. After toying around with "Net Objects Fusion" as a teenager, doing Websites for his friends and family he started an apprenticeship as a Media Designer in 2003 and gathered knowledge working in many large projects as a designer and front-end developer.

Nowadays, apart from designing and building stuff for everything that has a screen, he loves exploring new technologies, optimizing workflows for his team and just "nerding around". Also he has a strong interest in UX, UI, marketing and almost everything that is related to the web.

In his free time Felix spends most of his time with his family and his little son. He also is a huge fan of EDM and finds himself getting lost on soundcloud searching for new artists and tracks quite regularly.
